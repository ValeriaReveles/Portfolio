GameFace

GameFace is a social media platform that allows gamers to connect with other gamers in order to enhance their gaming experience. GameFace users are able to create and personalize their profile based on platform of choice, gamertags, and favorite games. Additionally, GameFace users are able to connect with other users via posts, likes, comments, and highlights. GameFace gives users the ability to find gaming partners based on games played, region, skill, and play style. Ultimately, GameFace is a networking solution that allows users to connect and grow as gamers in a safe and inclusive environment.
